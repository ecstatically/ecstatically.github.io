Features

Continuous Measuring

Measuring their social-emotional wellbeing once a semester or year is just not enough. We help your school/district by asking questions every day throughout the year so that you can better understand your students.

Not Distracting

We don’t constantly ask questions, instead we ask only one or two questions every day towards the end of a class to collect enough data for a statistically significant and confident result.

teachers love it!!

Friendly Chat Interface

Nobody likes filling out surveys, but we all love texting and chatting! Ecstatically is a fun, empathetic and intelligent chatbot that adds a personal touch when asking questions.

Data Privacy

We comply with FERPA, COPPA, SOPIPA and all student responses are anonymous for honest feedback. Schools and districts will be alerted if a student mentions harm or violence against others or themselves.

Real Time Administrator Dashboard

View qualitative and quantitative data analytics along with insights at any time across different surveys and questions and get breakdowns by grade, school, day, week, month and year. And, it’s beautiful (like you).

Custom Questions

Ecstatically comes with default surveys and questions out of the box. You can upgrade to Ecstatically Pro to have the ability to push out custom questions to students by grade and school.
