How Does It Work?

We’ll put a little Ecstatically magic on every student’s chromebook.

We’ll ask questions to students about their emotional wellbeing including questions about interactions with other students, engagement with coursework, bullying, stress, welcomeness, school safety etc.

View quantitative and qualitative data on different key metrics on the admin dashboard. This is the perfect way to learn more about how your students feel and what they want and take action accordingly.

Ecstatically also works with schools and districts to create action plans based on the data to create a better overall experience for students.

Features

Continuous Measuring

Getting feedback from students and measuring their emotional wellbeing once a semester or year is not enough. Ecstatically helps measure continuously so that you can better understand your students.

Not Distracting

Ecstatically doesn’t constantly ask questions to every student but rather only a few every week to collect enough data for a statistically significant average. We also don’t ask questions when the student seems to be working on class material.

Friendly Chat Interface

Nobody likes filling out surveys. Ecstatically is a funny, empathetic and intelligent chatbot that adds a personal touch and acts as a companion for students.

Anonymous Responses

All student responses are anonymous for honest feedback. A student does have the option to reveal their identity. Schools and districts will be alerted if a student mentions violence against the school or other students, self harm or suicide.

Gorgeous Administrator Dashboard

View qualitative and quantitative data across key metrics and get breakdowns by grade, week, month and year. Also, you can make comparisons between other Ecstatically schools and districts.

Question Customizability

Ecstatically comes with default metrics and questions out of the box. However, they can be customized and more can be added at the grade, school and district level.
